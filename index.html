cricket Information

Cricket is a sport played between two teams of 11 players, using a bat and ball. The goal is to score more runs than the other team.


Cricket Rules

  1. Field: The field is oval-shaped with a rectangular pitch in the middle.
  2. Wickets: Two sets of three wooden stumps with two bails on top are placed at each end of the pitch.
  3. Batting: The batting team tries to score runs by hitting the ball with the bat.
  4. Bowling: The bowling team tries to dismiss the batsmen by hitting the wicket with the ball.
  5. Innings: Each team takes turns batting and bowling, and each turn is called an innings.

Where it's Played

  • Bangladesh
